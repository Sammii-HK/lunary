## Brand Tone & Messaging Core

Everything stems from your tone: **grounded spirituality, emotional truth, and modern calm.**

### Keep:

- Sentence case for all posts (capitalize first letter of sentences)
- No em dashes, ever
- Rhythm from line breaks
- Vulnerability first, value second
- No hype. Authenticity > performance
