## Analysis notes

### Why CTR appears "low" but isn't

The overall 0.5% CTR looks alarming in isolation but is completely normal for this site's stage:

1. **Average position 12-16** — most content is on page 2 or lower, where benchmark CTR is 0.5-1.5%
2. **Massive impression growth** — Google is testing the site across thousands of queries, many at position 20+. These drive up impression counts without corresponding clicks.
3. **Long-tail queries dominate** — "cancer april 2026 horoscope" at position 8 has 12.7% CTR, well above the 1-3% benchmark. The individual query performance is strong.
4. **Google Dance phase** — the site went from 500 to 25,000+ impressions/day in 3 months. CTR always lags impressions during this phase.

**Expected trajectory**: As positions stabilise over the next 4-8 weeks, CTR should naturally climb to 1-2% overall as more queries settle into page 1 positions.
